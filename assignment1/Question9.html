Question 9: What other media forms (digital or otherwise) does the experience reference?

The aesthetic of the website seems to mimic a robotic or futuristic device interface. This is through the use of hexagonal buttons, screens, moving images and sliders. The colours orange, grey and silver also contribute to this aesthetic, creating a bright contrast and make the website look like the graphic interface of a robot. This allows the designers to be creative when incorporating interactivity, as buttons and minigames compliment the overall aesthetic.
