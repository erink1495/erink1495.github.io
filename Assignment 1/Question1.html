Question 1: What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

When first looking at the website, the overall aesthetic of the layout caught my attention. The colour theme used was very thematic, which created contrast and highlighted the important information on the website. The layout of buttons and interactive elements also caught my attention as it was thematic and very unique. The use of hexagonal buttons and what seems to be a receipt dispenser adds character to the website, making it fun and engaging.
